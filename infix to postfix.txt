1. Know the priority of operators.

operators: |, <, >, 2>, &&, ||, ;
order 1: <, >, 2>
order 2: |
order 3: &&, ||
order 4: ;
Or something like this...

2. No two operators of the same priority can stay together in the stack.

3. Lower priority cannot be placed after higher priority.
